SOUTH AFRICA'S WHITES AND BLACKS LAST WEEK CELEBRATEDTHE HOLIDAY IN IRONICALLY CONTRASTING WAYS . WHILE WHITES PICNICKED ORFROLICKED ON BEACHES, THOUSANDS OF BLACKS MOURNED THE ABSENCE OFRELATIVES WHO WERE EITHER BANISHED OR BEHIND BARS . UNDER THE COUNTRY'SMAZE OF WHITE-SUPREMACIST APARTHEID RULES, NON-WHITES MAY BE BANISHEDFROM URBAN AREAS TO DISTANT VILLAGES FOR A VARIETY OF CAUSES . EXAMPLE: WORKERS WHO HAVE BEEN IN A CITY FOR 20 YEARS OR MORE MAY BE SENTBACK HOME AT ONCE IF THEY LOSE THEIR JOB .
