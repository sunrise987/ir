LAST WEEK A YOUNG AUSTRIANOUTDID THEM ALL, SMUGGLING OUT HIS PRETTY FIANCEE AND HER MOTHERTHROUGH THE SIMPLE EXPEDIENT OF KEEPING HIS HEAD DOWN . HEINZ MEIXNER,20, HAD MOVED TO WEST BERLIN TWO YEARS AGO TO TAKE A JOB AS A LATHEWORKER . AS A FOREIGNER, HE WAS ABLE TO CROSS THE LINE FREELY INTO EASTBERLIN, WHERE, AT A STUDENTS' DANCE LAST SEPTEMBER, HE FELL IN LOVEWITH TINY, ATTRACTIVE MARGARETE THURAU . WHEN MARGARETE APPLIED FORPERMISSION TO EMIGRATE TO AUSTRIA, COMMUNIST POLICE TOLD HER THAT SHESHOULD MARRY HER YOUNG MAN IN EAST BERLIN AND SETTLE DOWN THERE . THISWAS MORE THAN MEIXNER COULD BEAR . " AS SOON AS I HEARD THAT, " SAYSMEIXNER, " I MADE UP MY MIND TO GET HER OUT . " LAST EXIT . HE LAID HISPLANS WITH METICULOUS CARE . TO GET A GOOD LOOK AT THE COMMUNIST SIDEOF THE FRIEDRICHSTRASSE CROSSING POINT FOR FOREIGNERS, MEIXNER STALLEDHIS MOTOR SCOOTER NEAR THE PEPPERMINT-STRIPED STEEL BEAM THAT CLOSESTHE LAST EXIT IN THE WALL . HIGH .
