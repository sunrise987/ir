THAT WAS THE STORY IN LONDON LAST WEEK WHEN GOVERNMENT ANDCOLONIAL LEADERS SIGNED THE BIRTH CERTIFICATE OF A NEW BRITISHCOMMONWEALTH NATION . IT WAS THE FEDERATION OF MALAYSIA, WHICH WAS TOBE COMPOSED OF INDEPENDENT MALAYA, SELF-GOVERNING SINGAPORE, AND THETHREE BRITISH TERRITORIES OF SARAWAK, BRUNEI AND NORTH BORNEO . BUT ATTHE LAST MOMENT, THE OIL-SODDEN SULTANATE OF BRUNEI PULLED OUT OF THEAGREEMENT IN A FIT OF PIQUE OVER THE FINAL TERMS OF FEDERATION . MACY'SV . GIMBELS . THOUGH THE TUNKU HADDEMANDED THAT SINGAPORE KICK IN 55 PER CENT OF ITS REVENUES TO THEFEDERAL TREASURY, LEE MANAGED TO WHITTLE THE FIGURE DOWN TO 40 PER CENT. BUT OVERPLAYING HIS HAND, HE THEN HELD OUT FOR 39 PER CENT . SOINFURIATED WAS THE TUNKU AT THIS MACY'S V . INLONDON LEE DEMANDED THAT THE BRITISH COMPENSATE SINGAPORE FOR CONTINUEDUSE OF THE ISLAND'S NAVAL AND MILITARY FACILITIES . BRITAIN CAMETHROUGH WITH AN OFFSHORE ISLAND AND AN OFFICERS' CLUB GOLF COURSE,WHICH LEE PROMISED TO TURN INTO A BOTANICAL GARDEN . BUT WHEN THENEGOTIATIONS TURNED TO SUCH BASIC MATTERS AS SINGAPORE'S CONTINUEDSTATUS AS A FREE PORT AND ITS FINANCIAL CONTRIBUTION TO THEUNDERDEVELOPED BORNEO TERRITORIES, THE DISCUSSIONS BOGGED DOWN .
